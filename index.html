Ask A Tech Event Organizer

In general, conference organizers have a wealth of siloed information AND often have no idea what non-conference organizers, starting conference organizers, or even other conference organizers might know or not know. In an effort to help break down the barriers between the shadowy, behind-the-scenes conference organizer and the wide world of attendees and organizer curious, we created this site as a place for you to ask and get answered whatever question you might have about tech events. Responses will be either made by Chris Williams (@voodootikigod) or an appropriate guest organizers and will be posted to http://www.voodootikigod.com with the title prefix of "AATEO" (for now, but this may change). If a question warrants a private response and an email is provided, we will email the response directly to you.
All responses are provided not as assertion, but as experience based recommendations. All participating organizers acknowledge upfront that there is no one, absolute correct way to organize a tech event and actually encourage you to create events in your own voice. The provided responses are based on the individual organizer's experience and not intended to be a final, one-size-fits-all solution to the question.
